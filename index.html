Boxer

Origin: Germany

Life Expectancy:  "9-10years"

Breed Speciality:  Boxers are intelligent, high-energy, playful dogs that like to stay busy. Their temperament reflects their breeding. They prefer to be in the company of their owners and are loyal pets that will fiercely guard their family and home against strangers. Few boxers bark excessively.

Origin: Northern Japan

Life Expectancy:  "10-12years"

Breed Speciality:  Akitas are muscular, double-coated dogs of ancient Japanese lineage famous for their dignity, courage, and loyalty. In their native land, they are venerated as family protectors and symbols of good health, happiness, and long life.
